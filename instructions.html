Groupify - FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Groupify

What is Groupify?

Groupify is a web app that helps you split members into balanced teams based on your preferences. Simply upload a CSV file, configure the settings, and let Groupify handle the rest.

What are the mandatory columns in the CSV?

The CSV must include the columns Name and Gender. These can be in any case (e.g., name, NAME) and will be automatically standardized.

How does Groupify handle gender values?

The app recognizes Male, Female, M, and F, in any case (e.g., male, MALE). All values are converted to lowercase for consistency.

What is the role of the department column?

The department column helps Groupify create interdisciplinary teams by ensuring members from different departments are evenly distributed. This field is optional, but including it enhances the diversity of the teams.

What if the CSV doesn't include the department column?

If the department column is not included, Groupify will still generate teams based on the available criteria like name, gender, and team size preferences. However, interdisciplinary team splitting will not be possible.

Are department names case-sensitive?

No, department names are not case-sensitive. Groupify automatically standardizes department names (e.g., CS, cs, and CSE will all be treated consistently).

Can I specify the number of boys and girls in each team?

Yes, you can set the minimum number of boys and girls per team in the configuration options before processing the CSV file.

How can I download the processed data?

Once the teams are generated, you can download the updated CSV file using the "Download" button provided on the main page.

Is my data secure?

Yes, Groupify processes your data locally in your browser. No data is uploaded to a server or shared externally.
